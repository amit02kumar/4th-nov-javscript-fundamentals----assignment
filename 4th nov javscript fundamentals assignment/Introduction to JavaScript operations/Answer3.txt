Unary Operator:-
(1)- This operator require one operand for operation.
eg:- typeof operator, delete operator,etc
typeof "amit";

Binary Operator:-
(1)-This operators require two operands for operation.
eg:- Arithmetic operators, comparision operators, etc.
let a= 10;  let b=5; let sum = a+b;

Ternary Operator:-
(1)-This operator require three operands.
eg:- conditional operator.
let score = 80;
let scoreRating = score > 50? "good" : "poor"
